1.

Join 3Dom Wraps

Sign Up to one of our plans depending on the service you require, or simply create a free account and try them all, and give us some details about you to make your experience of our products as effective as possible. Should you have any questions about our accounts, please see our pricing page or contact us for more details

2.

Create your project

Go through our Services page and chose the Sponsorship App. You will be asked some simple questions along the way, like upload your sponsors logos or use our own, to creating your first project. Projects are used to create your sponsorship package for your vehicles, clothing and products. You will have 1 project per item, where you can customise and save your progress.

3.

Load Sponsor Values

Once in the app with your chosen model, you can choose Sponsor Values app to price up the total value of that item to suit your sponsorship needs. For example if you are working on your Car Sponsorship package, you simply put in the total value you require from sponsors for the vehicle sponsorship package. This will then calculate the value of each area of the vehicle using our advanced research techniques.

4.

Load Sponsorship

You can now go to the Sponsorship app and position your logos on your model, depending where you wish each brand or sponsor to feature. When you do this, you will see a real time value for each logo appear. If you have 1 logo in an area, this will calculate the full price. once more than one logo features in each area, its size will determine the value compared to the other logos.

5.

Load Presentation app

Once you are happy with your logos and the value of your Sponsorship, you can move on to creating all of your stunning screenshots and images for your presentation. These will all save into your Gallery for this project and will be available to you when creating your Online Sponsorship Presentation.

6.

Follow the export process

Finally it is time to create your Online Sponsorship Presentation. SImply Export your project and follow the simple form process, select your screenshot images for inclusion in the Presentation until finally your presentation is complete.

7.

Get yourself noticed

Now its time to share your Presentation and 3D creations with potential sponsors. Share your screenshots on social media to build interest, or keep them private until you announce your success stories. Your presentation will come as a PDF to be emailed out directly from 3Dom Wraps and also as a web link that can also be sent via our 3Dom Wraps emailing system.

8.

Additional options

Now you have created your Presentation, you are well on the way to your racing season. Its time to get Quotes for your vinyl livery, find your transportation to the track, customise and order some team kit, create your marketing emails to keep your fans and sponsors up to date and begin working in your Advanced Race Dashboard. You can do all of this with us, check back to our Services page for more info. Your season starts now!
